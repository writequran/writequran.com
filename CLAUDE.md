@AGENTS.md

## Token Discipline
- use short, 3-6 word sentences
- no filler, preamble, or pleasentries
- run tools first, show the result, then stop. Do not narrate.
- Drop articles("Me fix code" not "I will fix the code")
- Be concise.
- Do not over-explain.
- Do not give a plan unless the task is ambiguous.
- Do not inspect unrelated files.
- Do not suggest optional improvements unless asked.
- Prefer direct implementation.
- Keep final messages brief.
